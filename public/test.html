Keeping Communities Safe Across the UK Rail Network

Working together to reduce risk, raise awareness and build safer railway communities.

Travel Safe Travel Smart supports communities across the UK by promoting railway safety, preventing harm and strengthening local partnerships. We work directly with schools, colleges, community groups, local authorities and partner agencies to help people understand the risks around the railway and encourage safer behaviour.

What We Do

Community Safety Managers work at the heart of local communities, delivering practical support and coordinated safety initiatives.

🎓

Education & Outreach

We deliver engaging, age-appropriate rail safety sessions to schools, youth groups and community organisations. Our sessions help young people understand railway risks and the impact unsafe behaviour can have on themselves and others.

Partnership Working

We work closely with British Transport Police, local councils, schools, charities and community organisations to respond to safety concerns and develop joined-up solutions.

Reducing Trespass & ASB
